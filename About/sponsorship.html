BMHC Sponsors

Black Magic Hockey Club is an all-volunteer non-profit organization that has proudly built a strong successful hockey association with deep roots in our communities. We rely on support from community members and area businesses to consistently deliver high quality hockey programs..

BMHC is a 501(c)(3) tax exempt charitable organization. Contributions to BMHC are tax deductible. Your employer may match your charitable contribution.

There are various sponsorship opportunities for sponsorship: Tournament and even sponsors, and Jerseys. Soon Dasher boards and Banners will be available for opportunities.

BMHC depends upon sponsorship and gifts from businesses and individual members of the community to fully fund its programs. Please contact sponsorship coordinators if you are interested in helping with sponsorship activities or known a company or individual who would like to sponsor.
