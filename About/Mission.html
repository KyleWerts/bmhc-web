Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission Statement

To promote hockey in a fun and safe manner. Parity among teams and sportsmanship will take precedence over winning in order to promote safe and competitive games. To develop and encourage participation in the sport of hockey by making the sport accessible to all who wish to play within the guidelines of the BMHC.

Vision

To be the premier non-profit organization for adult hockey development in our region, known for our welcoming community, dedication to player progression, and commitment to the highest standards of sportsmanship. We envision a future where anyone, regardless of their background or experience, can find their passion for hockey and a lifelong community on the ice.

Values

  • Community & Friendship: We believe hockey is at its best when it brings people together. We prioritize creating a positive, inclusive environment where friendships are forged and every player feels a sense of belonging.
  • Development & Progression: We are dedicated to providing the resources and coaching necessary for every player to progress, from their very first skate to mastering the fundamentals of the game.
  • Sportsmanship & Respect: We uphold the highest standards of sportsmanship on and off the ice. Fair play, respect for opponents and officials, and a positive attitude are the cornerstones of our culture.
  • Accessibility & Inclusivity: We are committed to making hockey accessible to all by removing barriers and ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to experience the joy of the game.
